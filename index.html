    About GT Food Plus

    Our story, mission, and values

    Our Story

    Founded in 2010, GT Food Plus began as a small family restaurant in Lagos with a passion for authentic Nigerian cuisine. What started as a humble kitchen serving neighbors has grown into one of the most beloved restaurants in the region.

    Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of customers daily, all while maintaining the homemade quality that started it all.

    Our Mission

    To bring the rich, diverse flavors of Nigerian cuisine to everyone while maintaining the highest standards of quality, hygiene, and customer service.

    We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, supporting Nigerian farmers and producers.

    Our Team

    Our chefs have decades of combined experience preparing traditional dishes exactly how they should be - with care, expertise, and love.

    From our kitchen staff to our delivery team, every member of GT Food Plus is dedicated to providing you with an exceptional dining experience.
